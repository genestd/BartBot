Chat Commands

Service Announcements

Get a list of current service announcements for the BART system

Connection Information

Find the next train between two stations

Example usage: When is the next train from 12th to 16th? 12th to 16th?

List Stations

List all station abbreviations for the BART system

Example usage: List all stations; All stops

Closest Station

Find the station closest to your location

Example usage: What's the closest station to 620 veterans blvd, redwood city? Closest station?

Weather Forecast

Get a the weather forecast at your closest station

Example usage: How is the weather in San Francisco? Forecast in Redwood City?
